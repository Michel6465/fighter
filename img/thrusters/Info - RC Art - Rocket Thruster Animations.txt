Reactorcore Art (RC Art)
==
PACK: Rocket Thruster Animations

--

Pixel-art sprites pack depicting animation frames for thruster exhaust flames of jetplanes, rocket engines and hover propulsion.

The package offers both sheets and individual images. Both are PNGs with a transparent background.

--

CC0, you can use these assets however you want, including commercial projects.

--

You can use Alferd Spritesheet Unpacker to quickly split the large atlas image into seperate individual images.
https://github.com/ForkandBeard/Alferd-Spritesheet-Unpacker

The animation spritesheets can be cut quickly into individual images with Sprite Sheet Slicer:
https://isometric8.itch.io/sprite-sheet-slicer
